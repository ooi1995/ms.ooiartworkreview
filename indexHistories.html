Career Histories Mrs. Ooi

1 March 2022 – Present

Now, I was in Troopers Innovation Sdn Bhd as a full-time Graphic Designer in the creative department.

2 February 2021 - 28 February 2022

On 2 February, I received an offer to work in the real estate industry as a graphic designer at the senior executive level. However, after a year, I was offered an opportunity in Troopers Innovation with an increment payment of 42.86% of my current salary.

7 September 2020 - 31 January 2021

7 September 2020, I was promoted to Senior Graphic Designer in the event and renovation industries, “Shock Wave Sdn Bhd”. Unfortunately, Movement Control Order 2 (MCO 2), affected me again by losing my job cause the company had financial issues.

13 July 2020 - 31 August 2020

However, on July 13, 2020, I restarted my full-time work at "Crid Studio Sdn Bhd" as a Graphic Designer, and after 2 months, I quit working here because the company did not comply with the country's law in giving employees EPF in addition to experiencing technical problems with my laptop.

18, 19, 29 June 2020

At the June 2019, I come back again as a part-time Graphic Designer at "Top Trident Traders Sdn Bhd". But I rejected their offer for joining full-time due to transportation problems.

5 August 2019 - 15 May 2020

On August 2019, I be hiring a full-time job as a Junior Graphic Designer at Event Industries, "EventWork Sdn. Bhd." On May 2020, Movement Control Order 1(MCO 1) due to covid-19 affected me by losing my job cause the company having financial issues with no business during MCO.

24 September 2018 - 23 January 2019

On September 2018, I started a 3-month industrial training in the printing service industry, "Mahir Digi Print Sdn Bhd", as a Graphic Designer Intern.

Start at July 2018

In July 2018, I started my first freelance job as a freelance graphic designer. My first client was just a small chicken rice.

2015-2014

In 2015, I received an offer to continue my studies at Universiti Utara Malaysia (UUM) as a Bachelor of Media Technology with honors in Digital advertising and graduated in 2019.

2013-2014

In 2013, I started my studies as a pre-university student at SMJK(C) Chung Hwa, and in 2014 I managed to receive an outstanding student in the STPM examination with a CGPA of 3.25.
